Paradise Island Snorkeling Day Tour

Enjoy unforgettable swimming on the private beach on Paradise Island and a relaxing boat trip on the Red Sea.

Paradise Island is a nature reserve in the Red Sea off Hurghada and offers its visitors white sandy beaches and colorful pristine coral reefs.

Swim and snorkel with an incredible variety of different fish and discover the unique underwater world.

Program of Paradise Island Snorkeling Day Tour

  • Boat trip with a snorkel stop
  • Stay on the island
  • Folklore show

Description for Paradise Island Snorkeling Day Tour

We will pick you up from your hotel in a fully air-conditioned vehicle and transfer you to the port where your snorkeling excursion begins. You get on board your boat, where you can choose a comfortable seat on the deck. There is both sun and shade on the deck. After the friendly crew have introduced themselves and briefly brief you on safety on board, a fabulous and interesting journey through the Red Sea begins for your snorkelling trip.

Admire the colorful corals and swim with a variety of exotic fish such as clown fish, butterfly fish, grouper, parrotfish and many more. Thanks to the crystal clear water, they are all perfectly visible. Also watch out for other marine life such as stingrays and turtles.

After a snorkeling stop, we will continue to Paradise Island, where we will arrive at around 12:00. There you will receive lunch (buffet) and have time to relax, swim or snorkel on the white sandy beach. There will be a folklore show for your entertainment around 14:00 and then you can relax a bit before we return by boat to the port at around 15:45. (earlier check-out in winter) where your transfer car will be waiting for you to take you back to your hotel.

Hurghada Private City Tour

Would you like to get to know the real, original Hurghada? Would you like to visit the tourist attractions of the city? Are you tired of mass tourism and coffee trips? Would you like to see, be amazed and really enjoy?

We have put together the right mix for you. Of course you also have the possibility to design your tour with us individually! Hurghada city tour with Memnon Reisen is away from mass tourism!

Program of Hurghada Private City Tour

  • Mosque El Mina Masjid
  • Coptic Cathedral of Saint Shenouda
  • The fish market
  • The vegetable market
  • Hurghada Marina Boulevard

Description for Hurghada Private City Tour

AT 14:00. we will pick you up from your hotel in a quality air-conditioned vehicle.

The first stop takes you straight to the highest point in the city. From here a beautiful panoramic view of the entire city of Hurghada and the Red Sea opens up.

Then we go to the city pier. Let yourself be enchanted by the beautiful yachts and the harbor atmosphere as you sail along a leisurely stroll. We continue our tour to the fish market. Next stop is the oldest church in the city, the Coptic Church and the largest seaside mosque.

From there we will drive to the vegetable and fruit market in the old town. We then drive to a local cafe to enjoy mint tea or Egyptian coffee.

If you have a special request, you can discuss it with our guide. We will stop at all interesting places.

Around 19:00. we will take you back to your hotel.

Our tour of Hurghada will be absolutely thrilling and you will get many new experiences from Hurghada.

Ride on Horseback through the Desert and Beach

Ride on horseback through the desert and beach to explore the great natural wonders of Hurghada on this horseback riding adventure.

Itinerary

We’ll pick you up at your hotel, Start your day with a horseback ride guided by a professional instructor. Ride for 1 hour through the desert and then another hour along the beach. Enjoy stunning views as your ride along the beach and make unforgettable memories in an unforgettable environment.

What to Expect

  • No need to worry if you’re a beginner, as helmets are provided and a trainer is on hand to coach you and provide assistance if you need it.
  • Enjoy a rare opportunity to ride an Egyptian horse in the sea and desert
  • Explore the desert on horseback
  • Play in the sea with your horse

Transfer Information

Transfer from any hotel in Hurghada is free. Transfer from (Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay, El Gouna) is charged extra €5 per person, and from (Soma Bay, Safaga), extra €10 per person.
