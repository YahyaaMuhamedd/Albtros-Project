Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride

This 3-hour tour is an extremely dusty adventure with absolute fun guaranteed!

The 3 Hour Safari by Quad Bike is a great opportunity to see the desert in all its beauty and splendor. The Safari by Quad Bike Tour is for those who want to cross the desert on a four-wheeled ATV.

During the Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride, you can explore the life and traditions of the Bedouin village. And also ride a camel and learn interesting facts about their life in the desert.

Memories and impressions of the excursion Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride will stay with you for a long time!

Program of 3 Hours Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride

Time Program
⏰ 7:30 Pick up from the hotel
⏰ 8:00 Arrival at Safari Station
⏰ 11:00 Departure to the hotel
⏰ 11:30 Back in hotel

Description for 3 Hours Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride tour

We will pick you up from your hotel at about 07.30 am and bring you to the Quad Bike training station. There you will receive instructions on how to drive your Quad Bike correctly and safely. And also make some test laps under the supervision of an instructor.

All Quad Bikes have an automatic control system, so in order to ride, you just need to apply the gas and the brake. Learning to drive a Quad Bike is not difficult at all, we are sure you will succeed!

We drive about 1 hour (approx. 25 km) with Quad Bike until we arrive to the Bedouin village.

Friendly Bedouins will meet and greet you in the Bedouin Village. They will be happy to show you how they live, tell you about their traditions, life and treat you to Bedouin tea.

In the same place you will have the opportunity to ride camels and explore the area. After that, you have to return on your Quad Bike and drive 25 km back to the Quad Bike training station from where we started.

After that, we will bring you back to your hotel.
